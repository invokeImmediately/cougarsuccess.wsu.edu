Helpful Apps

What WSU apps are available?

You may want to download mobile apps that can provide valuable information and services, including:

  • Get app – WSU Dining Service’s app that allows you to order food from your phone or mobile device
  • myWSU app – a mobile-friendly version of the myWSU platform
  • Washington State Cougars Gameday app – follow Cougar athletics schedules, live audio feeds, social media and more
  • WSU app – general information and news about WSU
  • WSU New Coug app – designed to help students keep track of Week of Welcome events
