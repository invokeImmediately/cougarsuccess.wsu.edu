About this Site

This website was designed as a “one-stop shop” for new or returning students looking for information about campus resources and life at WSU. The goals of this site are to increase retention and successful progress toward graduation of new and current WSU students; to address the varying needs of WSU’s diverse student population and growing community; to foster a culture of success amongst students; and to facilitate students’ development of strong academic foundations.
