Wellness

Many students encounter a rough patch, but they often do better after talking with their instructor, or from taking academic coaching or wellness workshops (drop-in hours available).

Check out the Academic Success and Career Center for help with academics, including drop-in tutoring. Find more resources to help with stressful situations.

  • We’re here for you. We want to help students navigate sudden and unexpected life events. If you haven’t already, we encourage you to contact your campus student services for a consultation on support we can provide. The Office of the Dean of Students can provide support and assistance for a wide variety of issues.
  • You may be able to get help with course materials from your instructor, the library, or from your campus student services office, with more resources at Student Financial Services.
  • Cougar Health Services at WSU Pullman offers physical and mental health services to students and many are covered by the Student Health Fee.
  • Students at WSU Everett may contact WSU Everett Health and Wellness Services
  • WSU Global Campus students may visit Wellbeing Online for a variety of health and wellness information and services
  • WSU Spokane students are welcome to visit Counseling Services
  • WSU Tri-Cities students may visit Campus Health and Wellness
  • WSU Vancouver offers Counseling Services for students
